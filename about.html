About Kitty Den


Caring for cats one paw at a time

The Kitty Den is a premium cat-only care company providing safe, enriching and stimulating cat care.

We deliver top-quality care, personalised attention and services that cater to the individual needs of each cat in our care. Our cat-loving team of dedicated staff are passionate about ensuring the health, happiness and well-being of every cat in our care. We pride ourselves on creating a fun and welcoming atmosphere where cats can play and socialise in a safe space.

Kitty Den History

Creating The Kitty Den:

How Megatron's Happiness Inspired Our Cat Care Services

Our cat, Megatron, seemed unhappy and lonely during the day while we were at work after the lockdown. We decided to build him a play den to improve his well-being, and he thrived in his new space. This led us to believe that other cats could benefit from similar care. Thus, The Kitty Den was born.

From Favor to Full-Service:

The Evolution of The Kitty Den's Cat Care Offerings

Our cat care services began as a favour to friends and neighbours. As word spread, more people requested our help, prompting us to expand our offerings to include overnight stays. To ensure the comfort of our feline guests, we've constructed snug cat dens.

Feline Socialisation at The Kitty Den:

Building Playrooms for Cat Interaction

As we took in more cats, it became clear that some of them enjoyed socialising with their feline peers. This prompted us to construct a playroom specifically for cats to engage in play and interaction with one another. Additionally, we created a separate daycare playroom for cats who enjoyed socialising during the day, offering them a stimulating environment to play in.

Collaborating for Cat Health:

The On-Site Care Den at The Kitty Den

We have taken steps to improve our care services by collaborating with a nearby veterinary team to establish an on-site care den within our facility. We aim to provide regular health assessments and specialised veterinary attention for cats in an environment that is less stressful for them.

Exceptional Care for Happy Cats:

The Mission of The Kitty Den

Today, we take pride in providing top-quality care, personalised attention, and exceptional services at The Kitty Den. Our mission is to ensure the comfort, happiness, and well-being of every cat that comes through our doors.
