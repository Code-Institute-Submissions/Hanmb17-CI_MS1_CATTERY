Meet The Resident Cats

Avocato

Avocato is a laid-back and easy-going cat who loves nothing more than a lazy day at home. Her favorite activity is napping in cozy spots around the den, but she also enjoys playing with string toys and batting around balls.

Megatron

Megatron is a playful and adventurous cat who loves to explore new places and meet new people. His favorite activity is to climb up high and survey his surroundings, but he also enjoys playing with toys and cuddling up with his humans.

Garfield

Garfield is a high-energy cat who loves to climb and play. His favorite activity is to race around the house and jump onto his jungle gym, where he can practice his acrobatic skills and show off his agility.
